**MapMindAI** is an open-source community building AI-powered SLAM tools for mapping, localization, and autonomous robotics, based in Hong Kong.

We combine classical estimation — LiDAR SLAM with **FAST-LIO2**, IMU/GNSS fusion with **ESIKF/ESKF** — with modern AI perception, including **Depth Anything**, **SuperPoint**, **LightGlue**, and **Gaussian Splatting**, to give robots a reliable understanding of the space around them.

Our work spans four areas:

* **SLAM & Mapping** – LiDAR-based mapping and calibration tooling for platforms like the Unitree Lidar L2
* **Visual Localization** – turning ordinary cameras, including old Android phones, into RGBD/VIO localization modules
* **Edge AI Inference** – Dockerized ONNX/TensorRT pipelines that run modern perception models on embedded hardware
* **Robot Control** – ESP32-based control and WebRTC streaming for low-cost, hackable robot platforms

Everything we build is developed in the open on [GitHub](https://github.com/MapMindAI), with the goal of making state-of-the-art robotics perception accessible to developers, researchers, and makers — not just teams with large robotics budgets.

Whether you're prototyping a robot, researching SLAM, or just curious how machines map the world, we welcome contributions, issues, and ideas on any of our repositories.
